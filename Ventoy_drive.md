# Ventoy setup guide

[Download link](https://www.ventoy.net/en/download.html)
or you can also use this 
[ventoy windows|Linux zips](https://github.com/abyd0w/Manual-Arch-Linux-Installation/tree/main/Ventoy)

1. Use the executable ventoy2disk
2. Choose your external drive ( make sure there is nothing in it, cause the drive will be formatted )
3. After its complete copy paste the iso files(DVDimages inside the drive named ventoy(the pendrive you formatted))

## How to use it?

- [ ] Reboot your computer
- [ ] Choose your ventoy drive as `boot option 1`
- [ ] make sure secure boot is off
- [ ] Save and exit and voila, choose your iso and happy installing....
